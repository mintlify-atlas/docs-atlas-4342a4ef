# Introduction to Bastion

Bastion is a powerful, feature-rich Discord bot designed to enhance your server experience. Whether you're managing a massive community with thousands of members or a private server with close friends, Bastion provides the tools you need to automate tasks, moderate effectively, and keep your members engaged.

## What is Bastion?

Bastion is a self-hosted Discord bot that gives you complete control over your server's automation and management. Built with Node.js and powered by Discord.js, Bastion offers a comprehensive suite of features including moderation tools, music playback, gamification, game statistics, AI-powered chat, and much more.

## Why choose Bastion?

### Self-hosted control
Unlike public bots, Bastion runs on your own infrastructure, giving you complete control over data, uptime, and customization.

### Comprehensive features
From moderation to entertainment, Bastion consolidates dozens of specialized bots into one powerful solution.

### Active development
Bastion is actively maintained with regular updates, bug fixes, and new features based on community feedback.

### Open source
The entire codebase is available on GitHub, allowing you to inspect, modify, and contribute to the project.

## System requirements

Before installing Bastion, ensure your system meets these requirements:

- **Node.js**: Version 18 or higher (LTS recommended)
- **MongoDB**: Version 4.0 or higher
- **Memory**: Minimum 512MB RAM (1GB+ recommended)
- **Storage**: At least 500MB free space
- **FFmpeg**: Required for music playback features

<Note>
Bastion can be deployed on cloud platforms like AWS, DigitalOcean, Heroku, or run locally on your own hardware.
</Note>
